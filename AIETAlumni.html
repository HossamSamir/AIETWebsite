Our Vision

The AIET Alumni Association is an organization that is to serve the Graduates of the Alexandria Higher Institute of Engineering & Technology. The role of the Alumni is to establish and enhance a continuing relationship between the AIET and its graduates.

Our Mission

Our mission is to serve the AIET Alumni interest and goodwill through the provision of a range of benefits and services and by encouraging intellectual and emotional ties between the Alumni members and AIET. In addition, it coordinates the work between the Alumni and the different departments in AIET to provide the best means to support the graduates.

Alumni Association

To raise the level of service provided to the member of the alumni, the Alumni Association will electronically publish periodical issues of our alumni publication on the AIET official website. Additionally, the Association will organize official events, reunions and job fairs for the alumni members .

Alumni Administration

The alumni administration will format the following specialized committees to supervise the alumni's activities:


The planning committee: aims to help the graduate to plan his future functional job also suggesting the necessary training programs incorporation with the Continuing Education and Training Center CETC, to keep up with the changes that happen in the marketplace, through the following training programs:


  • a. ICDL
  • b. Microsoft IT Academy Training Programs.
  • c. Cisco Networking Academy Training Programs.
  • d. Language and Translation Courses.

The sociality and communication committee: responsible for the communication with the graduates to inform them about the alumni's activities and determining the time of administrative board of the alumni working session.


The entertainment and athletics committee: Organize the entertainment and athletic meetings to the alumni members.


The journeys and cultural committee: Organize the journey's programs as well as the cultural seminars to the alumni members.
