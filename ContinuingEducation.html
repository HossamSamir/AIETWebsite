Continuous education & training department program



Our Vision
The Continuing Education and Training Center (CETC) aspires to be locally, regionally, and internationally recognized as one of the leading training centers that encompasses world-class level of training that aim to serve the needs of industry and business community of AIET graduates and the society.
Our Mission
This Continual Education and Training Center delivers continual education programs that links the AIET Institute and the community by offering multi-course certificates, which are designed for persons who want to acquire a thorough background in such fields as computer software and hardware, information technology, electronics and communication systems, motorcar electronics, foreign Languages, marketing, management, e-commerce and e-business.
